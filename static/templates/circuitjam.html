Welcome to Circuit Jam 24

What is Circuit Jam 24?

CircuitJam24 is a 24 hour hackathon organised by EHTP ElectroPower, Hassania's official electronics and robotics club.

The event takes place in Hassania School of Public Works in Casablanca and will host more than 30 teams from all over Morocco, do you have what it takes to compete?

How Can I participate ?

  • Gather a team of 1 to 5 people
  • Sign up by filling the form on our website
  • Assemble a robot according to the speciefied BOM (Bill Of Materials)
  • Submit a short video introducing the robot and the team members to either Tarik Ligabi (+212 708-017550) or Abdellah Radad (+212 656824030) on Whatsapp

What should I include in my robot in order to participate?

MPU6050 (Or equivalent)

Microcontoller

ESP32 or Arduino uno are strongly recommended

Infrared sensors (At least 4)

Will be used for line tracking

Optical encoders (2 are recommended)

Bluetooth module

2WD chassis (A small chassis is strongly recommended)

Rechargeable batteries

DC motor driver



Participants are allowed and encouraged to use components outside of the BOM.

When will the specifications booklet be revealed?

The specifications booklet will be revealed at the start of the event.
