Who are we?



Established in 2017, ElectroPower stands as the culmination of a legacy of ingenuity, building upon the foundation laid by several pioneering clubs with kindred spirits.

Dedicated to the fusion of electronics and passion, we are a collective of engineering enthusiasts driven by the thrill of unraveling the intricacies of software and hardware design. With every passing year, we endeavor to elevate our craft, empowering our members to soar to new heights of technical prowess.

Through a vibrant tapestry of events, visits, training classes, and workshops, we cultivate an environment where innovation thrives and skills flourish. Join us as we embark on a journey of discovery, where circuits become canvases for creativity and technology serves as our playground.

Together, let's amplify our potential and illuminate the path towards a future shaped by the power of innovation.

Welcome to ElectroPower - where we work to make the world brighter!
